1. Description of Service

Nur is an Islamic prayer times and worship companion app that provides prayer time calculations, Quran text and audio, Qibla direction, Azkar, Tasbeeh counter, Zakat calculator, Islamic calendar, and related features.

2. Prayer Time Accuracy

Prayer times are calculated using established astronomical methods (Adhan.js library) based on your location. While we strive for accuracy, prayer times are approximations and may vary from your local mosque's schedule. We recommend verifying with your local religious authority for critical timings.
