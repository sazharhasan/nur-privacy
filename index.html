Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Nur ("we", "our", or "the app") is an Islamic prayer times and Quran companion app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Location Data

Nur uses your device's GPS location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Your location is processed locally on your device and is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties. You can use the app with Makkah as a default location if you prefer not to share your location.

Anonymous Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how features are used and improve the app. This includes:

  • Which screens are visited
  • Which features are used (e.g., Quran reader, Qibla compass, Zakat calculator)
  • App settings (calculation method, juristic school)
  • City-level location (e.g., "London, UK" — not precise coordinates)

Analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. No names, email addresses, or personal identifiers are collected.

Subscription Data

If you subscribe to Nur Premium, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers

3. Data Storage

All your app data (settings, bookmarks, reading progress, Tasbeeh counts) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have a user account system and do not sync data to any cloud service.

4. Third-Party Services

  • PostHog — Anonymous analytics. PostHog Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Quran.com API — Quran translations. No personal data is sent.
  • EveryAyah.com — Quran audio recitations. No personal data is sent.

5. Children's Privacy

Nur does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is safe for all ages as it does not collect personal data.

6. Tracking & Advertising

Nur does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers. We do not display targeted ads. We comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data or maintain user accounts, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your app data is stored locally and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@crescententllc.com
